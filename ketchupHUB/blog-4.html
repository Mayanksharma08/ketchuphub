CAKE ME AWAY


I am a huge fan of chocolate, especially chocolate cake. I have been making this chocolate cake for 2-3 years now. It is probably the first cake I made and it is still my favorite.

It is a chocolate cake afterall!!!!!!......

The moist chocolate cake melts in my mouth, sticky with ganache on my tongue. With the rich deep shades of brown on every delicious layer this cake acts as a slice of happiness.

I am more of a dark chocolate fan rather than milk or white chocolate. For me it’s all about that slightly bitter flavor of dark chocolate that my taste buds crave for.

This chocolate cake will satisfy any chocolate lover's craving. The cake itself is dense and moist, just like proper chocolate cake should be.

And the frosting!!!!!!!!!!

Wowwwww…..
Pure chocolate that is heaven to me. It’s simple, consisting of chocolate, heavy cream and butter, which is basically how you make chocolate ganache.

It is a dreamy chocolate heaven right there.

A little bliss in every bite.

Hey, Batter! Batter!
If you like chocolate (off course you must do) you must try this mouth watering delicious chocolate cake.

I start by preheating my oven at 180°C for 10min. Sometimes I forget to preheat and then ended up waiting for the oven to be preheated.[i]

Till then I take a big bowl. (I don’t want things to be messy as I have to clean it by my own so its better to play safe).

Now I pour one cup of condensed milk in a bowl. As the condensed milk is flowing in the bowl I always dip my finger to taste the creamy, sweet condensed milk.
Now BEAT….. BEAT…… BEAT…… I sieve all the dry ingredients and mix them in the mixture of butter and condensed milk. (I will mention all the ingredients at the end).

At the same time add vanilla essence in this mixture. I just love its smell. It is strongly aromatic.
Fold gently everything and you will get smooth consistency. The texture and color of batter looks so wonderful.

Now transfer the cake batter in a greased tin and put it inside the oven at 180°C for 35-45min.

Frost, frost baby!
Now heat some cream in a saucepan and when it is about to boil just turn off the heat and pour this hot cream in a bowl full of small chopped pieces of darkchocolate. Cover it and keep it aside for sometime. After 2-3 min start mixing this mixture. Add little butter to make shiny ganache.
I just love this chocolate ganache so much that I can eat it all at once.

Refrigerate it for sometime and this thick, chocolaty ganache is ready to be spreaded on cake.

To maximize the ratio of chocolate ganache to cake, I cut each of the cake layers horizontally and spread the frosting all over it .

You can decorate cake as per your choice. I keep the decoration on this cake simple and classic. Because I like simple and elegant. I chose to cover this whole cake from chocolate ganache and some colorful sprinkles and Choco chips.

Yayyy…. Finally cake is ready.

Let me give you a warning…..
This cake is so good and so easy to make that you'll make it all the time and probably end up increasing your calories. But don’t worry you can compensate it later with your workout.

Stop hating and start baking
Go and try this amazing chocolate cake.
Ingredients:
For cake: 1cup condensed milk, 3/4th cup butter, 1.5 cup refined flour, 3tbsp Cocoa powder, 3/4thtbsp Baking soda, 1tsp Baking powder, pinch of salt.
For chocolate ganache: 200ml heavy cream, 400gms Dark chocolate, 1 tsp butter
