Story behind KetchupHub

NIKHIL PATHAK CEO, KetchupHub

  • It was evening time. I was going for my coaching classes and at the same time, I was feeling hungry. By the roadside, a street-food truck was serving some delicious food. But there was a lot of rush. I decided to have some food and gave an order for the Paneer Roll. I was in the queue and waiting for 25 min and then I recieved my order.
