Hungry Bird Cafe June 4,2019

Hungry???!....Visit hungry birds……


My city Gwalior is a developing home to numerous fast food outlets. Today I am going to talk about a best cafe in Gwalior which a foodie always wanted to go.

The name of the cafe is Hungry birds. This is my favorite place to eat out. I often go to this café with my friends and family members both for the delicious food they serve and for the healthy environment they offer.

It is situated near roshni ghar old high court Gwalior. One can easily reach there by a cab or by their own convenience.

It is located on a first floor of a building on which the name of café is written in large letters. When I visited there for the first time I thought it might be in a very small space. But when I step in, it was not only spacious but surprisingly well organized and elegant.

The interior decoration of the café is something that makes it stand out from the crowd. The interior decorations the paintings on the wall, the furniture, and the nice arrangement of the interior make it nice looking. I liked the minions and hulk that are painted on the walls.

And yaa!!!!!How could I forget this…… The roof-top!!!!!!!!!! It is my personal favorite as it gives a very clear view of surrounding nature and the clear blue sky. It feels so comfortable and relaxed to have food there.

This café offers a wide range of dishes and the prices are reasonable and food is served reasonably quickly contrary to some other places where you have to wait for a long for your food.

Talking more about food , the food is so much delicious and the appearance of food can make your mouth watering and the texture and taste of food is so amazing . When I visited that café last time I ordered chilly potatoes and they were so crispy and tasty . I liked the chilly potatoes most. Also the food is so much hygienic and I also love their plating.

If you are a foodie then you must visit this café. You can visit this café anytime you’ll always enjoy there.

There is a unique and new service in this café so I don’t have to wait so long for my food. This service is ketchup hub service whenever I visit this café I always order my food using ketchup hub. This is one of my favorite service in this café.

This café provides some special discounts to their regular customers. But when I visited for the very first time for a get together with my friends I ordered through ketchup hub and got special discounts.

Other than that the staff members are friendly, well mannered and well behaved. When I visited with my friends one of the staff member helped us to click our group photo . (I don’t know why I am mentioning this)..

They also asks for feedback. I am always satisfied with their food and services, so I always gives them a positive feedback.

All in all, I think it is one of the best café in gwalior city and dear foodies you must surely visit this café once.
