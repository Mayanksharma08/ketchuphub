Chicken!
The first thing that I am going to blabber about in this very first blog post ours is going to be about the stuff that is eaten the most in this whole world. Whether it is the countryside of the United States or the rural provinces of china or the lovely streets of Bengal chicken is arguably the universal comfort food and that would be an understatement.

Chicken is the food of the people not just because it tastes good but also it’s versatility you can do basically anything with chicken, fry it, boil it, grill it, roast it, its flavor profile and it’s ability to go with anything is what makes chicken the best food in the world .

Down to raw estimates

On average 5 million chickens are eaten everyday at a daily basis, that’s a huge amount of chickens!
The country with the most consumption of chicken is Australia. In Australia chicken sales surpasses the sale of every other type of meats. And if you’re wondering what other types of meat they consume you’ll probably be grossed out right now. Australians on average consume 205 pounds of chicken per person every year!
After Australia the sweet second spot goes to non other than the United states of America, the birth place of Fried Chicken and soul food (I swear I’m not drooling over while writing this article), but still when ever the sweet sound of fried chicken enters our ears and the smell of greasy oil hits that sweet spot in our nostrils and that sound of that crisp chicken skin! Ah that’s oughta give everyone a od orgasm, literally food porn. From the global chain KFC or be it the southern soul food mantra friend chicken aways hits the sweet spot for us (not for the heath reasons though). IF YOU WANNA EAT THE CHICK YOU GOTTA ROCK THE BELLY.

Even in India where most of the population are vegetarians we still rock the cage when it comes to consumption of chicken.After all we gave the world the ever so wonderful chicken tikka and tandoori chicken. I can go on and on about different famous chicken recipes in india but that would take me forever as there is a new recipe with chicken at the end of every street.
For college students chicken momos are the norm , for more fine dining stuff the Mughals left us with a million fantastic recipes thatyou can share with your date or an elegant showing off all the while maintaining the intricacies of fine dining. What I mean to say is chicken is and will remain a favourite for everybody. It’s for every culture every age and every event .What is the real thing is that chicken is the food of the people and that is it.
